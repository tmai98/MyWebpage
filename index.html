ENGR 1202-M02 Final Project

In this project, the goal was to simulate a group of engineers designing, creating, and displaying a product that they were assigned. The checklist was to design a prototype,
design it in a CAD software, we used CREO Parametrics, simulate the moving individual parts of the product, and create a webpage for the product where we could display and
advertise the product. The product assigned to my group was a pool skimmer, a product where it needed to be water-proof, buoyant, able to hook a vacuum hose to, and able to
automatically dispense 5 inch and 3 inch diameter chlorine tablets into a pool. To make that happen, there had to be exceptonal teamwork and dedication to complete the project.
